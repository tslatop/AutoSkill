1. [4-1-6深慢呼吸放松训练](心理咨询/Family技能/认知行为疗法/微技能/4-1-6深慢呼吸放松训练/SKILL.md)

3. [5-4-3-2-1正念感官觉察练习](心理咨询/Family技能/认知行为疗法/微技能/5-4-3-2-1正念感官觉察练习/SKILL.md)

4. [ABC Theory Psychoeducation Delivery](心理咨询/Family技能/认知行为疗法/微技能/ABC Theory Psychoeducation Delivery/SKILL.md)

   - 类型：psychoeducation
   - 适用条件：A structured, client-centered micro-intervention introducing the ABC model (Activating event, Belief, Consequence) to teach that emotional/behavioral consequences stem not from events themselves but from interpretations—establishing foundational CBT metacognitive awareness early in treatment.

11. [ABC模型引导式信念识别与命名](心理咨询/Family技能/认知行为疗法/二级技能/ABC模型引导式信念识别与命名/SKILL.md)
